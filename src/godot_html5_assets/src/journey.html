Our Journey

Key milestones and events in our fight for vacant land rights in Pahrump.

Milestones

Group Formation

2023

The Pahrump Vacant Land Owners group was formed on Facebook to unite property owners facing zoning challenges.

First Petition

2024

Launched a petition to local authorities requesting a review of zoning laws affecting vacant land owners.

Legal Consultation

2025

Engaged with legal experts to explore the possibility of a class action lawsuit against restrictive zoning practices.
