The Zoning Challenge in Pahrump

Many property owners in Pahrump, Nevada, face significant hurdles due to zoning laws that require connections to city utilities—utilities that are often unavailable in the areas where their land is located. This has left countless parcels of land undeveloped and unusable for residential purposes.

Our group, Pahrump Vacant Land Owners, is dedicated to raising awareness, exploring solutions, and advocating for changes that will allow property owners to fully utilize their land.

Welcome to Our Site

This website serves as a central hub for vacant land owners in Pahrump, Nevada. Here, you'll find detailed information on the zoning and water rights issues affecting our community, practical resources for navigating these challenges, potential solutions including advocacy and legal options, and ways to get involved. Whether you're seeking to understand the problem, explore permitted uses for your land, or join our efforts for change—including a potential class action lawsuit—we provide the tools and information you need to protect your property rights.
